Our Approach
Our focus is on identifying the gaps in the current quality assurance practices. Our QA services are completely customizable and we can help you in building your quality assurance process to offer additional support.
 The different phases in our quality assurance practices are:
Test Process Assessment
Obtaining a clear picture of the current QA practices, mapping the stakeholder’s expectations, creating Business Cases.

Designing Blueprint
Meeting with stakeholders for modelling transformation, designing the roadmap towards QA transformation and refining the transformational approach.

Getting a Proof-of-Concept
Selecting the implementation methodology, conducting a pilot test and monitoring the results and using results to tweak the implementation methodology.
Implementation
Determining the engagement model and budgeting, monitoring the implementation, continuous monitoring and process improvement and knowledge transfer to the local team.
